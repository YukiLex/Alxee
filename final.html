What We Do

Our Services

Two disciplines, one creative vision. We offer photography and design solutions tailored to your brand or personal needs.

Photography Services

  • Portrait & Headshots — Professional portraits that capture personality with depth and authenticity.
  • Product Photography — Clean, compelling images that make your products irresistible.
  • Event Photography — Every milestone and candid moment preserved forever.
  • Editing & Retouching — Refined post-processing that enhances without losing the real.

Graphic Design Services

  • Branding & Identity — Logos, palettes, and visual systems that define your brand voice.
  • Marketing & Advertising Materials — Print and digital assets designed to convert.
  • Digital / Social Media Graphics — Scroll-stopping visuals for every platform.
  • Packaging Design — Packaging that communicates quality before the product is even opened.

Who We Are

A Team of Creatives, Driven by Vision

Lumis Studio is a Filipino creative team that lives at the intersection of photography and design. We believe every image is a story and every brand is a world waiting to be built.

Whether we're freezing a fleeting expression through the lens or crafting a visual identity from scratch, we pour intention into every pixel. Our work is personal, purposeful, and always memorable.

We are five friends turned collaborators — each bringing a unique perspective to the table, united by a shared obsession: making beautiful things that mean something.
